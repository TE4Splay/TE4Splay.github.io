Turbview 2.0

Digital tool for the visualization of axial-flow turbines desings. Users can generate different geometries by varying key characteristic parameters and may observe the fluid flow paths, velocity diagrams and efficiency variations. This new version allows both "zero-dimensional" and "one-dimensional" analysis. This new 1D feature provides a fast pre-design tool for studying the evolution of aerothermal variables and turbine geometry.

CompreXTool

Micro-app for the visualization of axial-flow compressors under off-design conditions. Users can explore velocity triangles, blade cascades and compressors maps by adjusting key dimensionless parameters. The tool links geometry, aerodynamics and performance in a single interface, allowing useres to play online with mass flowor speed variations. Designed as an interactive support for learning, it offers a simple and intuitive way to understand the complex behavior of axial compressors.
